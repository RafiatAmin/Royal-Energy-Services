About

Renewable energy is a vital, and a rather abundant source of energy, which if properly tapped can reduce pressure on climate change. Royal Energy Services is an establishment aimed at encouraging the use of renewable sources of energy in various parts of Africa. It brings together the main actors involved in developing, and financing projects that protect the environment in rural areas of developing countries. To this end, we will assess communities and carefully analyse which of the energy resources can be properly utilised and partner with NGOs and other financial bodies targeted at achieving initiaties like ours to effectively carry out this project. Upon successful completion of each project, we, at Royal Energy Services take up the burden of educating and ensuring adequate usage of these interventions by members of the community. Creating awarenes to prevent defforestation for access to clearer and affordable energy.


Our dependence on fossil fuels amounts to global pyromania, and the only fire extinguisher we have at our disposal is renewable energy
