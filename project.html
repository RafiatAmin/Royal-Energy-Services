Location: Idi-araba, Mushin area of Lagos state

PROJECT TITLE : Provision of solar panels

At Royal Energy Services, we have embarked on another project to maximise renewable sources of energy in Idi-araba, Mushin area of Lagos State. After months of extensive research, we have discovered communities in this region have access to less than 50 hours of electricity out of a possible 168 hours weekly. To this end, solar panels were installed in three locations to serve as a test run, which turned out successful. Electric supply incresed to 150 hours a week. This is above 90%, and as such, we would like to carry on this project fully, when this is successfully achieved, it will be one less problem to worry out. At Royal Energy Services, we are committed to visualising the UN SDGs before 2030, and are well on our way towards achieving it. The focus is on Goal 7, access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all. We strongly believe that access to affordable modern energy will help improve other sectors of a nation's economy
Proposed Solution

Solar panels would be installed on the rooves of houses to channel energy from the sun to power electricity throughout the house

Energy from the sun is already used to power streetlights at night in various parts of the world

Lamps can also be powered using solar energy
